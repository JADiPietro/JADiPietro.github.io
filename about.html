About Me

Hi, I’m Jackie DiPietro — a Ph.D. Chemistry candidate specializing in materials, polymers, and surface science. My work focuses on how engineered surfaces can change real-world performance, from coatings and fabrication to characterization and testing. I grew up in New Jersey and earned my B.S. in Chemistry and Biochemistry from West Chester University, where I conducted undergraduate research with Dr. Kurt Kolasinski on porous silicon nanoparticles. I’m currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Chemistry at Penn State under the advisement of Dr. Benjamin Lear, where I study photothermal patterning of thermoset coatings.
