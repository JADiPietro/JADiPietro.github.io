National Meeting • 2024

ACS Denver fall 2024 meeting," Direct patterning of PDMS through controlled photothermal curing"

The combination of laser writing, and 3D printing techniques makes it easy to control where curing, and associated patterning, occurs. By incorporating carbon black with PDMS and applying laser irradiation in a controlled pattern, simultaneous curing and patterning can be achieved, eliminating the need for molds, and decreasing curing times.
